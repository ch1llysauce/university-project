Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

A four-year program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the theoretical foundations and applications of computing. The curriculum of UAT covers theories, algorithms, data structures, programming languages, software development, and computer systems.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

A four-year program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the concepts and applications of Information Technology. The curriculum of UAT covers programming, database management, software development, and network security, and it equips students with the ability to design, implement, and manage technological solutions in different organizational settings.

Bachelor of Science in Information System

A four-year program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the design, development, and management of information systems. Students will be able to analyze business needs, create innovative technology solutions, and manage information resources effectively. The curriculum of UAT covers database management, software development, system analysis, and project management.

History

University of Aurum Technology: A Legacy of Innovation Since 1970

Founded in 1970, the University of Aurum Technology (UAT) began as a pioneering institution focused on harnessing the power of emerging technologies. Established during a time of rapid advancement in computing and engineering, the university aimed to provide a cutting-edge education that bridged the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

From its humble beginnings with a single building and a small cohort of dedicated students, UAT has grown into a world-class institution, offering a wide range of programs in engineering, information technology, business, and sciences. In the early years, UAT became renowned for its forward-thinking approach to education, emphasizing hands-on learning, research collaboration, and innovation.

Over the years, the university expanded its facilities, invested in state-of-the-art laboratories, and fostered partnerships with major corporations and research institutions. Today, UAT stands as a leader in technological education, research, and innovation.
